    • Who are We?

    A team of computer enthusiasts who want to make the world more personal and interactive. Social media are a major source of distraction during exam-preparation time. Our solution is to create a service that will lock people out of their Facebook, Twitter and other accounts. Users will have the option to access their accounts at any moment, but we will establish a monetary incentives not to do so before an initially agreed period of time has passed.

    Process

    The implementation is a straightforward process. First, an user signs in our service and they enter a period after which they want to receive their social media profile back. The user then surrenders their credentials for the given social media. Our service logs in the user's account and changes those credentials with new ones. At any moment users can request their access back, however, they will have to pay a fine to do that. In a later implementation, the money could be pledged in advance and returned after the deadline if the user hasn't requested their credentials back. We are using etherium wallet for the financial transactions because it is easy to implement and can support smart contracts in the future. However, we are going to look for ways to offer as many payment options as we can to make joining LockMeOut easy and convenient.
